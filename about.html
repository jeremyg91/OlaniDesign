Our Story

Family-Owned. Locally Built.

Olani Designs is a family-run business on Hawaiʻi Island, built around two people who love creating — Joe Lopez and Jeremy Gutsohn.

Joe grew up on Oʻahu and now lives on the Big Island with his family. He brings years of experience in foundations, concreting, framing, and finish work, and is the lead for our construction projects.

Jeremy is from Brisbane, Australia and moved to Hawaiʻi with his family. He specializes in custom signage, CNC work, and hardwood pieces — turning customer ideas into real, high-quality signs using materials like mango, mahogany, koa, and cedar.

Together, Joe and Jeremy combine construction skill and creative sign-making to offer homeowners and local businesses a one-stop, trustworthy team. You can be confident that your project will be completed with care, clear communication, and real professionalism.

Our Mission

To bring island ideas to life — whether that’s a full build, a renovation, or a custom sign — using durable materials, honest workmanship, and a creative approach that reflects Hawaiʻi.
